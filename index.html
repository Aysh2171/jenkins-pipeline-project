Our Heritage

Founded by Enzo Ferrari in 1929, Scuderia Ferrari has been the pulse of top-tier motorsport for nearly a century. From early Grand Prix battles to modern Formula 1 eras, our team forged a legacy of innovation, courage, and unrelenting pursuit of victory.

Major Achievements

  • 16 Constructors’ Championships
  • 15 Drivers’ Championships
  • Over 240 Grand Prix victories
  • Iconic drivers: Ascari, Lauda, Schumacher, Vettel, Leclerc

Every car we field, including the SF-25, carries this storied tradition onto the grid.

The Spirit of Ferrari

Scuderia Ferrari stands for Italian pride, engineering excellence, and a passion for racing that transcends generations. The SF-25 is not just a race car — it’s a statement: win, always strive, and never compromise.

“To build a Ferrari is easy. To race one is what makes it immortal.” – Enzo Ferrari
